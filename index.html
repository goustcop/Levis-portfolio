About me

I create bold social media and brand visuals for creative brands that want a memorable, high-energy look. My work lives at the intersection of maximalism and intention — super graphic design, unconventional layouts, and color that refuses to be ignored.

I've been obsessed with design since middle school, teaching myself brand identity, motion graphics, and social media visuals long before I landed my first client. Since then I've worked with film studios, fashion brands, startups, and nonprofits — building visual identities that stop the scroll and start conversations. Whether it's a full brand book, social content, or motion graphics, I design for brands that are tired of playing it safe.

KEWL Clothes

May 2026

KEWL Clothes is a creative fashion brand built around customization, thrift culture, and self-expression — making fashion feel personal, imperfect, and fun. I developed the full brand identity including logo system, color palette, typography, social templates, and brand guidelines. The identity is bright, unapologetic, and unmistakably KEWL — maximalist by design, intentional in every detail.

The brand operates three main pillars: the Kewlification Station (a pop-up customization booth at markets and thrift stores), original KEWL apparel, and a brand startup program for emerging creators.

About me

In a world full of AI design and instant graphics, I put the human touch back into everything from every day, to specialized creative projects you get excited about and ask your friends to join you for. Hello and welcome! I'm Levi, a graphic designer and artist of over 5 years. I create more than graphics, I create an experience for your audience and give them the attention and effort they deserve.

I've been obsessed with brand identity, artistic pursuits, and graphic design since middle school, which grew into an ever-evolving need to learn and adapt to the creative landscape around me. Since then I've worked with film studios, theatrical productions, TTRPG companies, and more. I build visual identities that spark conversation and community. Be it a full brand book, social media content, or motion graphics, I design for brands to create a world around their product and be more than just a couple of PNGs. Join me and take a look at all the cool stuff I've done. Can't wait to work with you!
